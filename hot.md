## Key Decisions
<!-- Recent trade-offs and choices that affect future work -->
- **Monthly timeframe added**: 3-way merge with regime-adaptive weights (trending 0.10d/0.50w/0.40m, ranging 0.60d/0.30w/0.10m, volatile 0.25d/0.45w/0.30m). When monthly is absent, remaining weights are renormalized to sum to 1.0.
- **Calibrator 17 features**: 6 category + 6 raw + 3 EMA spreads + weekly_score + monthly_score. Monthly_score = 0.0 during cold start (ridge handles gracefully).
- **~60 monthly bars** from 5 years of data; EMA-50 on monthly will be NULL for first ~50 months ŌĆö acceptable, scorer handles None indicators as 0.0.
- **Confidence base uses calibrated_score** (warm): `min(abs(cal), 8.0) * 10`. Cap at 8.0 ŌĆö accuracy drops above |cal|=8 (57.6%) and |cal|=12 (47.7%) due to calibrator overfitting. Cold start: `abs(final_score) * 0.3`.
- **`final_score` column is always ┬▒100** (merged timeframe composite). `calibrated_score` (Ōēł ┬▒2ŌĆō15%) is separate.
- Rolling ridge calibrator: window=365, lambda=0.1, 17 features. Validated R┬▓=0.1025 (was 0.0972 with 16 features) across 15 diverse tickers. Window increased from 90ŌåÆ365 to use ~14,400 training rows (was ~3,600); older data risk mitigated by staying within 1 year.
- Anti-predictive categories (candlestick, structural, sentiment) zeroed in adaptive weights.
